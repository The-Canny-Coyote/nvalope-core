Nvalope Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · Applicable: Worldwide

Nvalope is a privacy-first budgeting app operated by The Canny Coyote LLC. This policy describes how we handle information in plain language.

What we do not do

  • We do not collect your budget data, transactions, or personal information on our servers.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, or third-party tracking.
  • We do not sell or share your data with anyone.

Where your data lives

On your device: Your budget, envelopes, transactions, settings, conversations with Cache the Coyote (our AI companion), and any data you enter are stored only in your browser (IndexedDB and localStorage). They stay on the device you use. We do not have access to them.

Backup: If you choose a backup folder (Settings → Data Management), the app writes backup files to that folder on your device or computer. No copy is sent to us or any other server.

Bank connections (optional)

If you use the “Pull from bank” feature: You connect to your bank through a trusted third-party provider (e.g. Plaid). We do not receive or store your bank credentials, account numbers, or a permanent link. When you pull, we receive transaction data from the provider and pass it to your device so you can choose what to import; we do not store that data on our servers. Only what you import is stored locally on your device. Each pull is a one-time connection. See our Terms of Use and the provider’s privacy policy for more detail.

What we might receive (only if you choose)

Donations: If you support us via Buy Me a Coffee or similar, that platform may share your transaction with us (e.g. supporter name or message). We do not use that for marketing; we use it only to thank supporters and improve the app. Those platforms have their own privacy policies.

Minors

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone. Because we do not collect data on our servers, we do not have data to delete on request from a parent or guardian; any data is only on the user’s device and can be cleared there.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will change. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Your rights (e.g. GDPR / CCPA)

We do not collect or process your personal data on our servers. Your budget and app data stay on your device. So typical “data subject” rights (access, correction, deletion, portability) are in your hands: you can view, edit, or delete your data in the app, clear browser storage, or remove backup files from your chosen folder. If you have questions, use the Contact section below.

Contact

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, open an issue in the project repository or contact The Canny Coyote LLC via the project’s preferred channel.

The Nvalope public core app is open source (MIT). You can inspect the public core code to verify that no budget or personal data is sent to our servers.
